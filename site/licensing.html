Before you buy

Enter your panel's serial

We attach it to your order so the licence is issued for the right panel. Find it on the panel's Settings screen, or in the integration's Device → Licence card (with a Copy button). A serial looks like G051112302021105160.

Optional here — you can also type it on the Stripe checkout page. Entering it now just pre-fills your order.

Renewals. There's no grace period, so a licence that lapses brings the watermark back on the next draw. Home Assistant exposes a days_remaining value and the licence card turns amber under 30 days — automate a reminder on it.

Questions

Does it work offline?

Yes. The licence is verified on the device from a stored token — the panel boots licensed with the broker and Home Assistant switched off.

What if I have several panels?

Each panel is a separate device with its own serial, so it needs its own licence. Buy one per panel.

Can I start with one page and add more?

Absolutely — that's what single-page licences are for. You can also upgrade to Full later.

How do I apply the licence?

After purchase you receive a licence token. Paste it into the integration's Device → Licence card and press Apply. The watermark clears on the next draw.
